To save the Earth from pollution, consider adopting the following practices:
Understand and Prevent Causes: Recognize the activities that lead to pollution and take steps to mitigate them. 
1
Adopt Eco-Friendly Habits: Avoid bottled water, reduce food waste, and switch to LED lightbulbs. 
1
Choose Sustainable Transportation: Opt for biking or walking instead of driving for short distances to reduce air pollution. 
2
Engage in Community Initiatives: Participate in local clean-up efforts and support policies aimed at reducing pollution. 
1
Plant Trees and Gardens: Create bird and bee-friendly gardens to promote biodiversity and improve air quality. 
1
By implementing these strategies, you can contribute to a healthier planet.
