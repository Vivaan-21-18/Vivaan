The Solar System

The solar system includes the Sun everything that orbits it -planets, moons, asteroids, and comets. The Sun is at the center and gives light and heat.

Planets

  • Mercury-closest to the Sun
  • Venus-very hot and cloudy
  • Earth-our home with life
  • Mars-called the red planet
  • Jupiter-largest planet
  • Saturn-has beautiful rings
  • Uranus-tilted and cold
  • Neptune-farthest and windy

Other Objects

  1. Moons-orbit planets
  2. Asteroids-rocky bodies
  3. Comets-icy and have tails

The solar system is a big and exciting place. We are always learning more about it!
