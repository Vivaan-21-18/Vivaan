Soaring Dreams 


 Dr APJ Abdul Kalam, former president of India, was an inspiring leader and motivational speaker.
 In July 2015 he collapsed while delivering a lecture and passed away on July 27.
 However, his words remain relevant even to this day and continue to guide and motivate us.
Here are a few best-known poems written by him. In memory of Missile Man of India:
Famous air show of Paris in motion
My thought too in flight and yearns for my nation
When will planes designed in my land
Pierce the sky as lightening in action
And gracefully land as angels in full bloom
All to the envy pf spellbound spectators
Yes we can!!
When we are united in action and addicted to deeds
Sky can't be the limit for my nation in action

-A.P.J.Abdul Kalam
