Winter Carnival Food and Drinks List The following is a list of food and drink items that are commonly enjoyed at winter carnival events, providing a taste of the festive spirit and indulgence: Maple Taffy: A Canadian classic, hot maple syrup poured onto snow and lapped up with a popsicle stick. 1 BeaverTails: Hot fried dough topped with sugar, hazelnut spread, and other sugary delights. 1 Gajar Ka Halwa: A sweet treat made with carrots, almonds, cashews, and pista, especially popular in the Northern regions of India. 1 Skyu: A Ladakhi soup-like dish made with veggies and wheat dough, served with meat. 1 Gushtaba: A Kashmiri dish made with minced mutton balls slow-cooked in spices, often served at the end of a meal. 1 These items are not only delicious but also a way to celebrate the winter season and the festive atmosphere of winter carnival events.
