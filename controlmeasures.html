Here are some earth control measures (ECM) to manage and protect the environment effectively:
Erosion Control Blankets: Use erosion control blankets or canvas sheets to protect earth stockpiles and bare slopes. 
1
Drainage Management: Implement proper ECM to ensure efficient drainage systems, preventing floods and maintaining clean waterways. 
1
Silt Discharge Reduction: Apply best practices such as sealing hoarding footing, providing adequate silty water treatment capacity, and creating holding ponds to reduce silt discharge from exposed earth surfaces. 
1
Guidelines for Stakeholders: Engage qualified professionals to design and implement ECM, ensuring that measures are adequately reflected in project tenders. 
1
Water Conservation Tips: Incorporate practices that conserve water through site reuse and recycling to enhance environmental protection. 
1

These measures are essential for effective environmental management and construction practices.
